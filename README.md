### 4. Marketing Campaign Analytics Dashboard
**Industry:** Digital Marketing & E-commerce  
**Objective:** Track marketing campaign performance across channels and optimize ROI

**Features:**
- Real-time campaign metrics (91.12M impressions, 9.05M clicks, 8% conversion rate)
- ROI tracking by location (Chicago, Houston, LA, Miami, New York)
- Channel performance analysis (YouTube, Website, Email)
- Campaign type comparison (Social Media, Search, Email, Display, Influencer)
- Target audience segmentation (age groups, demographics)
- Monthly trend analysis with date filtering

**Key Metrics:**
- $81.96K total ROI with 97% growth
- $12.50K acquisition cost
- Multi-channel attribution tracking

**Technical Skills:** Power BI, Marketing Analytics, Multi-dimensional Analysis, ROI Calculation, Interactive Dashboards
